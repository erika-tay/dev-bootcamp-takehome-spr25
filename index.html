About Me

Hi! I'm Erika. I am a second year computer science major at Georgia Tech from Sugar Hill, Georgia. I am passionate about volunteering in my community for social good. In college, I have volunteered with China Care's monthly playgroups, which introduce Chinese culture to young adoptees in the Atlanta area. I volunteered at the Mid-Autumn Festival event organized by Tzu Ching chapters and the True Love organization to support kids with special needs. In high school, I taught elementary school students how to code. I love getting to know others and spending time with my family and friends.
